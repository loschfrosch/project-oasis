Green the planet and fight the climate crisis

More and more organizations are aiming to get carbon neutral. Land and forest restoration are proven practices to increase soil carbon. Oasis connects carbon sequestration actors to organzizations, who are ready for investing in CO2-removal.

Why should we care?

The news on climate change keep pouring in, it doesn't look too good.

  • Climate change threatens human life on earth
  • The climate crisis needs to be addressed urgently
  • Soils hold huge potential to sink CO2

What can we do about it?

The world is ready for forest and landscape restoration. Research shows a trillion trees could be planted. Oasis wants to help move ecosystem restoration to the center of economic activities. This happens when labour is service for the climate and when consumption is investment in climate mitigation or adoption.
