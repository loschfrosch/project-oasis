A marketplace for climate action

Soil depletion, deforestation and erosion leads to desertification of landmass. Healthy soils are a foundation for nutritive food and human existence on earth.

  • Soil degradation: 40% of earth’s total farmland
  • Erosion: 10-40 times accelerated by humans
  • Desertification: 10–20% of drylands lost to desert
  • Deforestation: 130,000 square kilometers per year
  • Food security: 870 million chronically undernourished

Now, what can we do about it?

Oasis is a marketplace for people who bring live to dead land, soils and forests and transform them into fertile, carbon capturing landmass.

Our current economic system is not well aligned with climate action. Oasis wants to help move climate productivity to the core of economic activities. This happens when human labour is service for the climate and when human consumption is investment in climate mitigation or adoptions.

Oasis connects actors of climate mitigation: Producers of carbon sequestration or removal can offer their services. Consumers can invest in climate actions by buying units (tokens) of said services.
